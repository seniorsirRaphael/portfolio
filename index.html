About Me

My journey in digital empowerment

My Mission

As an Applied Computer Science student at Egerton University, I'm passionate about bridging the digital divide in Kenya. My mission is to empower individuals and businesses with the digital tools and knowledge they need to succeed in today's technology-driven world.

My Story

Growing up in Maungu, Taita Taveta, I witnessed firsthand the challenges many Kenyans face in accessing digital services. This inspired me to dedicate my skills to helping others navigate government services, academic processes, and business opportunities online.

My Vision

I envision a Kenya where every citizen can confidently access digital services, from government applications to online business opportunities. Through my services, I aim to make digital literacy accessible to all.
